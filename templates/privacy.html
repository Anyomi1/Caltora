Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary: Caltaura processes call-related information to provide an AI receptionist and message capture service for businesses.

Information we collect

  • Account info: username, password (stored as a secure hash), and onboarding details you provide.
  • Business settings: business name/type, greeting, FAQs, timezone, and optional notification email.
  • Call data: caller phone number, called number, call timestamps, and speech-to-text transcript if enabled by your telephony provider.
  • Usage data: basic logs for troubleshooting and security.

How we use information

  • Provide the Caltaura receptionist features (answer calls, capture messages, show call logs).
  • Improve reliability, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Respond to support requests and service communications.

AI processing

If you enable AI features, Caltaura may send limited call context (such as the caller’s request and your FAQs) to an AI provider to generate a response. We instruct the AI to be concise and not invent facts.

Sharing

Caltaura uses third-party infrastructure providers (for example, hosting and telephony) to operate the service. We share only what is necessary to deliver the service.

Data retention

Call logs and transcripts are retained as needed to provide the service and support your business workflows. You may request deletion of your account data subject to legal and operational requirements.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring your use of Caltaura complies with applicable laws (including call recording/consent rules and customer notice requirements).
